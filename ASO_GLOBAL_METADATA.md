### ENGLISH ────────────────────────────────────

**SafeGuardian AI** — the most complete AI-powered safety companion for seniors and their families.

Whether your loved one lives alone or in assisted care, SafeGuardian AI gives you peace of mind 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

──── CORE FEATURES ────

🚨 **Emergency SOS**
One tap sends an instant emergency alert with GPS location to all family members simultaneously. Our AI engine detects unusual patterns and can escalate automatically.

💊 **Smart Medication Manager**
Never miss a dose. Set flexible schedules with voice confirmation. AI camera scans pill bottles and identifies medications automatically.

🤖 **AI Health Analysis**
Track blood pressure, heart rate, glucose, weight, and mood. Our AI identifies trends and generates easy-to-understand health summaries for the whole family.

👨‍👩‍👧 **Family Dashboard**
Real-time view for up to 10 family members. See last check-in, health status, and medication adherence at a glance — no account required for family.

🎙️ **Voice Assistant**
Large-button, voice-activated interface designed for seniors. Commands like "I took my medicine" or "I feel dizzy" are understood and acted upon instantly.

📊 **Health Records**
Secure cloud storage of all health data. Share records directly with doctors. Unlimited history on Premium.

🛡️ **Privacy First**
All data encrypted in transit and at rest. Full GDPR & HIPAA-aligned design. We never sell your data.
