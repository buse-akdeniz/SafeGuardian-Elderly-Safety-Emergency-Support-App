📞 SUPPORT & TROUBLESHOOTING
=================================================================

Port Already in Use:
  killall -9 dotnet

Build Failures:
  dotnet clean
  dotnet build --configuration Debug

Service Worker Not Registering:
  1. Check /wwwroot/sw.js exists
  2. Clear browser cache
  3. Unregister old: navigator.serviceWorker.getRegistrations()

Voice Recognition Not Working:
  • Check browser support: 'SpeechRecognition' in window
  • Requires HTTPS in production
  • Check microphone permissions
  • Verify lang='tr-TR' support in browser

LocalStorage Full:
  • Check size: navigator.storage.estimate()
  • Clear old logs: localStorage.setItem('debugLogs', '[]')
  • Max 1000 logs default (configurable)
